My Teaching Philosophy

I believe that every student has the capacity to excel in mathematics when it's presented in an engaging, relevant way. My approach focuses on building mathematical confidence through hands-on activities, real-world applications, and creative problem-solving.

Mathematics is not about memorizing formulas but about developing a mindset for logical thinking and pattern recognition that serves students in all areas of life.
